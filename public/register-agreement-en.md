# Platform User Registration Service Agreement

Pursuant to the Civil Code of the People's Republic of China, the Personal Information Protection Law of the People's Republic of China and other relevant laws and regulations, the platform operator and the registered user, in line with the principles of equality, voluntariness, fairness and good faith, hereby enter into this Agreement regarding the user’s registration and use of the platform, which shall be jointly abided by both parties.

## 1. Effectiveness and Scope of Application
1. This Agreement shall take effect on the date when the user completes the platform registration process, selects the user category corresponding to its entity type and clicks to confirm acceptance of this Agreement. It shall become a valid legal document binding the rights and obligations of both parties in relation to platform use.
2. This Agreement applies to all registration and use activities of the user on the platform. Any operation conducted by the user through the platform shall be deemed as full understanding and acceptance of all terms of this Agreement. Overseas users confirm that they acknowledge and comply with relevant laws and regulations of the platform’s location (China), as well as cross-border data and tax rules of their own jurisdiction. Legal liabilities arising from differences in jurisdictional rules shall be borne solely by the user.
3. Differentiated provisions for domestic and overseas individual users set forth herein are an integral part of this Agreement, and the corresponding user type shall be exclusively bound by the relevant clauses.

## 2. Platform Registration and Account Use
1. The user shall select a user category based on its real entity type. Any change to user information shall be updated on the platform in a timely manner; all adverse consequences arising from failure to do so shall be borne by the user.
2. The user shall properly safeguard its platform account, login password, verification code and other authentication information, and bear full responsibility for all operations conducted via the account and the resulting consequences. The platform operator shall not be liable for losses caused by account disclosure, theft or other reasons not attributable to the platform operator.
3. The user undertakes that the registration and use of the platform account comply with laws, regulations and platform rules, and shall not use the account to engage in illegal, rule-violating or acts contrary to public order and good morals. The platform operator reserves the right to suspend, freeze or cancel the user’s account and pursue legal liabilities for violations. If an overseas user violates Chinese laws through illegal acts, the platform operator will cooperate with Chinese judicial authorities in case handling and report relevant information to competent departments in accordance with regulations.
4. The user shall not change its entity type without authorization. If a change is necessary, the user shall submit an application and supporting documents to the platform operator, and the change may only be made upon approval by the platform operator.

## 3. Points Rules
1. The platform operator calculates and issues points based on the user’s contributions on the open-source platform. Points are divided into withdrawable points and non-withdrawable points. Specific calculation standards and classification of points shall be published on the platform. The platform operator reserves the right to make reasonable adjustments to such standards based on platform operation needs, and shall notify users via platform announcements following any adjustment.

### a. Withdrawable Points
Withdrawable points are subject to differentiated rules based on the user’s entity type and jurisdiction, as detailed below:
1. **Domestic individual users**: May apply for withdrawal in accordance with the proportion published on the platform. After confirming specific withdrawal information, the user shall sign a withdrawal agreement with the third-party platform designated by the platform operator. Specific provisions shall be subject to platform-published rules and the results of the third-party agreement signing.
2. **Overseas individual users**: May apply for withdrawal in accordance with the foreign exchange conversion ratio published on the platform (subject to the daily exchange rate of the platform operator’s cooperative bank). A legal overseas receiving account under the user’s own name is required. The platform operator does not withhold or pay relevant taxes and fees. Overseas users shall independently comply with tax rules of their jurisdiction, complete tax declaration and payment, and bear liabilities for non-compliance. Foreign exchange settlement fees for withdrawal shall be borne by the user, with specific standards subject to platform publication.

### b. Non-withdrawable Points
Non-withdrawable points may only be redeemed for electronic rights, vouchers and other platform-specified benefits in the platform’s points mall, and may not be withdrawn as cash. Points are non-returnable and non-refundable upon redemption. The validity period and usage rules of non-withdrawable points shall be subject to platform publication.

2. The user shall not obtain points through illegal means such as cheating, false submission or malicious brushing. Upon discovery of such conduct, the platform operator reserves the right to immediately revoke all illegally obtained points, suspend or terminate the user’s points usage rights, and cancel the user’s platform account in serious cases.

## 4. Information Collection and Use
### a. Basic Information Collection
To complete core services including platform registration, points withdrawal and rights redemption, the user authorizes the platform operator to collect information voluntarily provided during registration and platform use, including but not limited to name/entity name, identity information, contact address, contact phone number, email address and bank account information.

### b. Open-source Platform Information Collection and Contribution Calculation
The user voluntarily authorizes the platform operator to collect relevant information of public accounts on open-source platforms such as GitHub and Gitee (including but not limited to code submission records, project participation records, Issue/PR interaction records and repository information). The platform operator may calculate the user’s open-source contributions in accordance with platform-published rules based on the collected information, and issue corresponding points. The user confirms that such authorization is a true expression of intention and that it holds legitimate rights to the provided open-source platform account information.

### c. Scope of Information Use
User information collected by the platform operator shall only be used for agreed services including platform registration, points calculation, points withdrawal and rights redemption, as well as necessary scenarios for improving service quality and optimizing platform functions. Without the user’s written consent, such information shall not be used for purposes beyond this Agreement (except as required by laws and regulations).

### d. Encrypted Storage of Information
The platform operator undertakes to implement encryption storage, access control and other security protection measures in compliance with Chinese laws, regulations and industry standards for collected user information, establish a sound information security management system, and employ professional security technicians to prevent information leakage, tampering and loss, ensuring the security of user information.

## 5. Account Information Analysis and Data Usage Authorization
1. The user agrees and authorizes the platform operator to conduct compliant analysis, data integration and desensitization processing on platform account information and the aforementioned open-source platform account information, in strict compliance with Chinese laws and regulations on information protection.
2. The user authorizes the platform operator to use aggregated/anonymous desensitized and anonymized data (which cannot be associated with the user’s personal identity either alone or in combination with other information and does not constitute personal information under the law) for legitimate purposes including platform operation analysis, open-source ecological research and industry analysis.
3. The user agrees that the platform operator may provide, transfer or sell the aggregated/anonymous data specified above to cooperative customers with legitimate needs. Such acts do not constitute disclosure of the user’s personal privacy information, and no separate user consent is required. Cooperative customers shall use the data in compliance with the agreement with the platform operator.
4. The platform operator undertakes not to provide, transfer or sell non-desensitized and non-anonymized user account information and personal information to any third party (except as required by laws and regulations or with the user’s written consent).

## 6. Rights and Obligations of Both Parties
### 6.1 Rights and Obligations of the Platform Operator
1. The platform operator has the right to collect and analyze user information, calculate and issue points, and take corresponding measures against user violations in accordance with this Agreement and platform-published rules.
2. The platform operator shall ensure normal user access to platform services and points benefits, maintain the compliance and stability of points withdrawal and mall redemption, respond to reasonable user inquiries and complaints in a timely manner, provide technical support for cross-border withdrawal for overseas users, and coordinate with cooperative banks to complete foreign exchange settlement.
3. The platform operator shall strictly fulfill confidentiality and security protection obligations for user personal information and account information, implement effective measures to prevent information leakage, and bear compensation liability in accordance with the law if user losses arise from information leakage due to the platform operator’s fault.
4. The platform operator reserves the right to make reasonable adjustments to the terms of this Agreement, points rules and platform service content based on laws, regulations and platform operation needs. Adjustments shall be notified to users via platform homepage announcements, in-site messages or other means. Continued use of platform services by the user shall be deemed acceptance of the adjusted content.
5. The platform operator has the right to verify the authenticity of the user’s entity type and information, require the user to supplement relevant supporting documents, and suspend platform services for users with false or unverifiable information.

### 6.2 Rights and Obligations of the User
1. The user has the right to use platform services in accordance with this Agreement and platform rules, obtain and use legally calculated points, and put forward reasonable opinions and suggestions on platform services. Overseas users have the right to request the platform operator to provide Chinese and English versions of platform service rules.
2. The user guarantees that all provided information is true and valid, and bears full responsibility for the legality and authenticity of its open-source platform account information. The user shall bear compensation liability for losses incurred by the platform operator or third parties due to false information.
3. The user acknowledges and agrees to the clauses on information collection, analysis, data use, transfer and sale set forth herein, and shall cooperate with the platform operator in completing necessary information verification.
4. The user shall keep confidential the platform operator’s trade secrets (including but not limited to platform rules, data interfaces, operation strategies and cooperative customer information) acquired during platform use, and shall not disclose or use such secrets for any third party without authorization.
5. The user shall promptly notify the platform operator upon discovery of account information leakage, abnormal points or other irregularities, and cooperate with the platform operator in verification and handling.

## 7. Personal Information Protection and Authorization Boundary
1. User personal information referred to herein includes but is not limited to identity information, bank account information, precise contact address, unpublished contact information and other personal information that can identify the user either alone or in combination with other information. The platform operator shall bear strict confidentiality obligations for such information.
2. The platform operator’s use of user personal information shall be limited to the service scenarios specified in Clauses 3 and 4 of this Agreement. Without the user’s written consent, such information shall not be used beyond the agreed scope (except as required by laws and regulations).
3. If the platform operator is required to disclose user personal information by mandatory provisions of laws, regulations, judicial authorities or administrative organs, it shall cooperate within the scope of legal obligations and notify the user of relevant circumstances in a timely manner (except as prohibited by laws and regulations).
4. The user has the right to inquire, correct and delete personal information at the designated platform entrance, and may withdraw authorization for information collection and use as agreed herein. Withdrawal of authorization may result in inability to normally use platform services including points calculation and withdrawal, and the user shall bear all consequences arising therefrom.

## 8. Amendment and Termination of the Agreement
1. The platform operator may amend the terms of this Agreement based on updates to laws and regulations or platform operation needs. Amendments shall be publicized via platform announcements and take effect upon expiration of a publicity period of no less than 3 working days. Users who do not agree with amended terms may cease using platform services and cancel their accounts; continued use shall be deemed acceptance of the amendments.
2. The user may apply for cancellation of the platform account at any time. Prior to account cancellation, the user shall settle outstanding matters including undrawn points and unredeemed benefits. This Agreement shall terminate automatically upon account cancellation. The platform operator will retain user information in accordance with legal requirements and delete or anonymize such information upon expiration of the retention period.
3. The platform operator reserves the right to unilaterally terminate this Agreement, cancel the user’s account, recover illegally obtained points and benefits, and pursue legal liabilities if the user commits any violation of this Agreement.
4. Upon termination of this Agreement, the platform operator’s obligation to protect user personal information and the user’s obligation to keep the platform operator’s trade secrets confidential shall survive the termination and continue to be performed.

## 9. Dispute Resolution
1. Any dispute arising from the conclusion, performance or interpretation of this Agreement shall first be resolved through friendly negotiation. If negotiation fails, either party may file a lawsuit with the people’s court having jurisdiction over the location of the platform operator.
2. During dispute resolution, except for disputed matters, other terms of this Agreement shall remain in effect, and both parties shall continue to perform their respective rights and obligations.

## 10. Miscellaneous
1. Matters not covered herein may be supplemented by platform-published rules and announcements, which shall have the same legal effect as this Agreement. An English version shall be provided for overseas users.
2. If any clause of this Agreement is deemed invalid, revocable or unenforceable, it shall not affect the validity and enforceability of other clauses.
3. This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the platform operator and the user regarding platform registration and use, superseding all prior oral or written agreements between the parties relating to such matters.
4. This Agreement is made in duplicate, with the platform operator holding one copy and the user retaining one electronic copy, both having equal legal effect.
