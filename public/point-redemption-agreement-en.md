# Points Redemption Service Agreement

In accordance with the Civil Code of the People's Republic of China and other relevant laws and regulations, through equal and voluntary negotiation on points redemption, hereby enter into this Agreement based on the principles of equality, voluntariness, fairness and good faith, for mutual compliance and performance.

## 1. Points Issuance and Objection Handling
1. All withdrawable points and non-withdrawable points under this Agreement shall be issued by corporate partner users after review in accordance with their own rules. The specific quantity, recipients and time of points issuance shall be determined by such corporate users. Only technical channels for points issuance are provided, and there is no participation in the review or decision-making of points issuance by corporate users.
2. If there are objections to the quantity, time, type or other matters of points issuance, an objection application shall be directly filed with the corporate user that issued the points and resolved through negotiation. Assistance will be provided in communicating with the points issuer. Only the obligation of assisting communication is borne, with no participation in the handling or judgment of the objection dispute. The final dispute resolution result shall be subject to the negotiation opinion between the user and the corporate user.

## 2. Points Types and Redemption Scope
1. Points under this Agreement are classified into withdrawable points and non-withdrawable points, which may only be used in accordance with this Agreement and platform-published rules. They are not transferable across platforms. Points themselves do not have the nature of legal tender and shall not be used for illegal activities such as cashing out or gambling.
2. Withdrawable points: Cash withdrawal may only be applied for at the published ratio and shall not be used for other redemption purposes. Non-withdrawable points: Designated products/rights in the online points mall may only be redeemed and cash withdrawal shall not be applied for. The two types of points are not convertible to each other.

## 3. Rules for Withdrawable Points Redemption
### 3.1 Withdrawal Conditions
1. Cash redemption of withdrawable points is processed at a fixed ratio of 1 point = RMB, which shall be published in real time. If adjustment is needed, notification shall be given 7 working days in advance via platform announcement, in-site message or other means. The adjusted rules shall take effect upon the expiration of the publication period.
2. The quantity of withdrawable points applied for withdrawal shall not be less than the published minimum withdrawal limit. There is no maximum limit for a single withdrawal. If the balance of withdrawable points in the account is insufficient for the minimum limit, it may be accumulated until reaching the standard before application.

### 3.2 Withdrawal Application and Review
1. A withdrawal application shall be submitted through the designated entrance, including the quantity of points for withdrawal and confirmation of the provided bank account. The application is irrevocable once submitted.
2. Review shall be completed within 3 working days after the application is submitted, including the legality of points source, consistency of account information, compliance with withdrawal limits, etc. Approved applications shall be included in the monthly batch transfer list. For rejected applications, the rejection reason shall be promptly notified via platform message. Reapplication may be made after correcting the information. Corresponding operations shall be completed through the designated third-party platform for withdrawal application, and the final cash shall be disbursed by the third-party platform.

### 3.3 Batch Transfer and Arrival Time
1. The designated third-party platform shall process withdrawal transfers on the fixed day of each month (postponed to the next working day in case of legal holidays or bank system maintenance). All withdrawal applications approved in the current month shall be transferred to the provided bank account in a unified manner.
2. After the transfer operation is completed, the fund arrival time shall be subject to the actual settlement time of the bank where the provided bank account is located. No guarantee is made for a specific arrival time limit. No liability shall be borne for delayed arrival caused by third-party reasons such as bank system failure or settlement rules.

### 3.4 Bearing of Bank Fees
1. Bank transfer fees (including but not limited to inter-bank transfer fees, handling fees, settlement fees, etc.) incurred when processing batch transfers of points withdrawal shall be borne by the user.
2. Bank fees shall be subject to the actual amount charged by the bank and shall be directly deducted from the withdrawal amount. Actual amount received = Pre-tax withdrawal amount − Taxes payable − Bank fees.

## 4. Rules for Non-Withdrawable Points Redemption
### 4.1 Redemption Channels and Product Scope
1. Non-withdrawable points redemption may only be processed through the official online points mall. No authorization is given to any third-party platform/institution to provide relevant redemption services.
2. Products redeemable with non-withdrawable points are digital products/rights listed in the online points mall. Specific redeemable products and required points shall be subject to real-time display in the mall. The right is reserved to adjust product categories, inventory and points requirements in the mall according to operational needs without prior notice.
3. Products in the online points mall are available on a “first-come, first-served, while stocks last” basis. No liability shall be borne for compensation if redemption fails due to product shortage.

### 4.2 Redemption Operation and Review
1. After selecting a product for redemption in the points mall, the operation shall be completed as prompted. Upon confirmation of redemption, the corresponding quantity of non-withdrawable points shall be immediately deducted from the account. Points are non-refundable once deducted.
2. After points deduction, the product redemption code, usage link, activation method, etc. shall be immediately sent to the mobile phone/email address bound to the account.

### 4.3 Product Usage and After-Sales Service
1. Usage rules, validity period, applicable scope and other information of digital products/rights redeemed shall be subject to the official instructions of the product issuer. Only points redemption channels are provided, and no responsibility is borne for product usage guidance or after-sales support.
2. Redeemed digital products/rights shall be used within their validity period. Failure to use them upon expiration shall be deemed as automatic waiver. No reissue, extension or refund of points shall be made.
3. Once issued, redeemed digital products/rights are non-returnable and non-transferable unless there are statutory quality defects or the issuer explicitly permits return or exchange. No request shall be made to refund the deducted points.

## 5. Rights and Obligations of Both Parties
### 5.1 Rights and Obligations
1. The right is reserved to monitor the entire process of points acquisition and use. If abnormal points sources are found (e.g., inconsistency with corporate issuance records, suspected illegal brushing), the right is reserved to suspend redemption/withdrawal services and require proof of points source. If proof is not provided or the proof is invalid within the time limit, the right is reserved to permanently freeze relevant points. The right is reserved to review points redemption applications in accordance with this Agreement and reject applications with illegal points sources, abnormal account information or violation of platform rules, and freeze relevant points.
2. The right is reserved to adjust the points withdrawal ratio, minimum withdrawal limit and points mall products according to laws, regulations and platform operation needs, and notify after adjustment.
3. Responsibility is borne for ensuring the normal operation of the points redemption system and online points mall, conducting regular maintenance and upgrades, and giving advance notice (except for emergency maintenance).
4. Confidentiality obligation is borne for personal information, provided bank account information and other private information, and no disclosure to any third party (except as required by laws and regulations or with written consent). Security measures such as encryption and access control are adopted to ensure information security.
5. Consultation and after-sales support related to points redemption are provided, questions raised are replied to within 24 hours, and system failures and operational problems during redemption are resolved; assistance and communication services for points issuance objections are provided in accordance with this Agreement.
6. The right is reserved to require truthful provision and timely update of bank account information related to points withdrawal, and reject withdrawal applications for false account information provided. If the bank account information is frozen, closed, reported lost or in other abnormal status, the right is reserved to suspend withdrawal services until valid account information is updated. Losses such as fund interest and delayed arrival during the suspension period shall be borne.

### 5.2 Rights and Obligations
1. The right is reserved to apply for cash withdrawal of withdrawable points or redeem digital products/rights corresponding to non-withdrawable points in the points mall in accordance with this Agreement and platform-published rules.
2. The right is reserved to inquire about points balance, points redemption records, bank fee deduction details and other information, and apply for review of disputed records.
3. Truthful and accurate provision of bank account information related to points withdrawal, ensuring that the account information is true, valid and consistent with real-name authentication information. If the bank account is changed, reported lost, closed or otherwise modified, immediate update shall be made. Otherwise, consequences such as withdrawal failure, fund return or non-arrival shall be borne.
4. Ensuring that points are obtained legally and not through illegal means such as cheating, false transactions or malicious brushing. If the above acts are committed, the right is reserved to freeze and revoke all points, terminate this Agreement, recover withdrawn funds or redeemed products, and pursue legal liabilities.
5. Bearing all losses caused by abnormal bank accounts, failure to update account information in a timely manner and other reasons, and timely use of redeemed digital products/rights within the validity period.
6. Properly keeping platform account, login password, verification code and bound mobile phone/email address, and bearing full responsibility for all points redemption operations conducted through the account. Points losses and fund risks caused by account disclosure, theft or other reasons shall be borne.
7. Abiding by this Agreement and all published rules, cooperating in points redemption review and information verification, and not maliciously attacking or tampering with points redemption system and points mall data by technical means.

## 6. Liability for Breach of Contract
1. If false bank account information is provided or account information is not updated in a timely manner, resulting in withdrawal failure, fund return or additional bank fees, all losses shall be borne. If losses are suffered due to false information, full compensation shall be made.
2. If false information is provided, points are obtained through illegal means or points are used for illegal activities, liquidated damages shall be paid (twice the amount corresponding to the illegally obtained points). If the liquidated damages are insufficient to cover losses, the difference shall be compensated. The right is reserved to freeze and revoke all points, terminate this Agreement, recover withdrawn funds or redeemed products, and report to relevant authorities. When recovering withdrawn funds/redeemed products, the right is reserved to deduct other points or require return in cash.
3. Unless otherwise agreed in this Agreement, if either party breaches this Agreement and causes losses to the other party, compensation shall be made for actual losses (including but not limited to direct losses, attorney fees, litigation fees and other rights protection expenses).

## 7. Force Majeure
1. Force Majeure referred to in this Agreement means objective circumstances that are unforeseeable, unavoidable and insurmountable, including but not limited to natural disasters, wars, government acts, bank system failures/maintenance, telecom operator failures, cyber-attacks, epidemics, etc.
2. If unable to perform obligations under this Agreement due to Force Majeure, notification shall be given within 24 hours after the occurrence of Force Majeure and a Force Majeure certificate shall be provided within a reasonable period. The affected party shall be partially or fully exempted from liability according to the impact of Force Majeure, except as otherwise provided by law.
3. The party suffering from Force Majeure shall take all reasonable measures to reduce losses. If reasonable measures are not taken resulting in expanded losses, liability shall be borne for the expanded losses. After the elimination of Force Majeure, performance of obligations under this Agreement shall be resumed in a timely manner.

## 8. Dispute Resolution
1. Any disputes arising from the execution, performance, interpretation of this Agreement shall be settled through friendly negotiation first. If negotiation fails, either party may file a lawsuit with the people’s court having jurisdiction.
2. During dispute resolution, other clauses of this Agreement shall remain in effect, and both parties shall continue to perform their respective rights and obligations. Disputes regarding points issuance shall be resolved through negotiation or legal channels. No joint liability shall be borne for such disputes.

## 9. Miscellaneous
1. This Agreement shall take effect on the date of confirmation of acceptance of this Agreement. If there are no operations (including login, redemption, withdrawal) for 12 consecutive months, the right is reserved to list the account as a dormant account and suspend points usage rights. If the period exceeds 24 consecutive months, the right is reserved to clear remaining points in the account in accordance with platform rules without prior notice.
2. The Points Rules, Withdrawal Operation Details, Points Mall Management Rules and other published contents are integral parts of this Agreement and have the same legal effect. In case of any inconsistency between platform rules and this Agreement, the later one shall prevail.
3. For matters not covered herein, a supplementary agreement may be signed separately, which shall have the same legal effect as this Agreement.
4. This Agreement is made in duplicate, with one copy held by each party (electronic version for individual users, which has the same legal effect as the paper version), both having the same legal effect.
